The Best AI Tools for Teachers in 2025

Updated: December 22, 2025 • By Mustafa Alhaj Mustafa

Teaching is one of the hardest jobs in the world. AI is finally here to help lighten the load, automating the paperwork so you can focus on the students.

1. MagicSchool.ai

MagicSchool is the ultimate Swiss Army knife for teachers. It can generate lesson plans, write IEPs, create rubrics, and even compose emails to parents—all tailored to your specific grade level and subject.

2. Curipod

Turn a boring slideshow into an interactive experience. Curipod uses AI to generate slides with polls, word clouds, and open-ended questions that students can answer on their devices in real-time.

3. Brisk Teaching

Brisk is a Chrome extension that lives right in Google Docs. It can detect AI-generated assignments, give feedback on student writing in seconds, and change the reading level of any article to match your students' abilities.

Conclusion

AI won't replace teachers, but teachers who use AI will replace those who don't. These tools are giving educators their evenings and weekends back.
