10 Must-Have AI Chrome Extensions for Productivity

Updated: December 22, 2025 • By Mustafa Alhaj Mustafa

You don't need to open ChatGPT in a new tab every time you want to use it. These extensions bring AI capabilities directly to the webpage you are viewing.

1. Harpa AI

Harpa checks prices, summarizes web pages, and can even write replies to emails. It's a Swiss Army knife that lives in your sidebar.

2. Grammarly GO

More than just spellcheck, Grammarly GO helps you rewrite entire paragraphs to sound more professional, friendly, or concise with a single click.

3. Monica

Monica acts as a copilot for your browser. It can translate text on images, summarize YouTube videos instantly, and help you draft content on any website.

Conclusion

Installing these extensions is like giving your browser a brain upgrade. They save seconds here and there, which adds up to hours every week.
