Create a Winning Resume with AI in 2025

Updated: December 22, 2025 • By Mustafa Alhaj Mustafa

The job market is tough. AI resume builders help you beat the Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and get your CV into human hands.

1. Rezi

Rezi is laser-focused on ATS compliance. It formats your resume perfectly so that no robot rejects it because of a weird font or column. It also writes bullet points for you based on your job title.

2. Teal

Teal is more than a resume builder; it's a career growth platform. It tracks your job applications and suggests keywords to add to your resume based on the specific job description you're applying for.

3. Kickresume

Kickresume uses GPT-4 to write your resume from scratch. If you have writer's block and don't know how to describe your experience as a "Barista," it will generate professional-sounding bullet points in seconds.

Conclusion

Your resume is your marketing document. AI ensures it's optimized, keyword-rich, and error-free, giving you the best chance of landing that interview.
