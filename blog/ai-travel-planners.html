Best AI Travel Planners for Your Next Trip

Updated: December 22, 2025 • By Mustafa Alhaj Mustafa

Planning a trip usually involves 50 open tabs and hours of research. AI travel agents can build a complete itinerary—including flights, hotels, and restaurant reservations—in seconds.

1. Roam Around

Built on top of ChatGPT, Roam Around asks you where you want to go and for how long. It then generates a day-by-day itinerary with specific sightseeing spots and food recommendations.

2. TripPlanner.ai

TripPlanner goes a step further by helping you find optimal routes and estimating costs. It's great for road trips or multi-city European tours where logistics can get complicated.

3. Google Gemini (Google Maps Integration)

Google's AI can now plan trips by pulling real-time data from Maps and Flights. You can ask "Plan a weekend in Tokyo for under $1000," and it will check real flight prices and hotel availability instantly.

Conclusion

AI takes the stress out of travel planning, letting you focus on the excitement of the journey rather than the headache of the logistics.
