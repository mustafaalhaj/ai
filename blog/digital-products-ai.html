Creating E-Books and Courses with AI Assistance

December 14, 2025 • By Mustafa Alhaj

Knowledge commerce is a billion-dollar industry. The barrier to entry was always "time"—writing a 200-page book is hard. AI removes that barrier.

Creating an E-Book in 48 Hours

  1. Brainstorming: Ask AI for "15 burning questions beginners have about [Topic]."
  2. Outlining: "Create a 10-chapter outline that takes the reader from beginner to intermediate."
  3. Drafting: Feed the AI one chapter at a time. "Write chapter 1 based on this outline. Use a motivational tone."
  4. Polishing: You must read and edit. AI can be repetitive. Add your personal stories and examples—this is what makes it unique.

Course Creation

For video courses, use AI to write the lecture scripts. Then use teleprompter apps while you record, or use an AI Avatar (Synthesia/HeyGen) to present the content if you are camera-shy.

Marketing

The book is just the product. Use AI to write the sales page, the email launch sequence, and the social media ads to sell it.
